AhaKey Studio Quick Start
=========================

1. Drag "AhaKey Studio.app" into the Applications folder.
2. Open the app and allow Bluetooth access when macOS asks.
3. Turn on your AhaKey keyboard and keep it nearby.
4. In the app, click "连接设备" and wait for the keyboard to connect.
5. Keep Mode 0 selected for your first test.
6. Click the key, light bar, screen, or toggle you want to inspect.
7. Make a small change and click "同步到键盘".

Notes
-----

- Mode 0 is the official vibecoding preset.
- Mode 1 and Mode 2 are user-custom modes.
- OLED currently supports animated content preview first.
- If you enable AI status lighting later, the app may install a lightweight background helper.
